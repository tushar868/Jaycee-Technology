Electronic Rotating Paddle Sensor

Download Literature

The electronic rotating paddle sensor is used to detect the level of powder material either inside a machinery, or a bunker, or even a silo that is operating for its production. We at Jaycee Technologies Pvt. Ltd offers the finest quality and high-performing electronic rotating paddle sensor that is preferred for determining the lower and upper levels in solid, bunker, tank, and silo.

Electronic Rotating Paddle Sensor with Motor Fault Indication

We offer electronic rotating paddle sensors of various series that are manufactured to detect and be in charge of the liquid level inside a tank. You can use our offered sensor to detect all types of levels in containers, bin hoppers, and storage tanks to prevent loss of materials and over-spillage. Our electronic rotating paddle sensor features a foldable paddle to simplify installation procedure, rope probe for elaborate lengths, etc. Get in touch with us to place your order.
