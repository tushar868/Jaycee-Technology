Aqua Fork Point Level Switch

1. HONEYBEE 7210 - 2Khz frequency, Weather proof IP 67Cast Al housing. With universal mains. 18-30 V Dc and 65 to 265 V AC. 1 DPDT relay output.

2. HONEYBEE 7220 - 2Khz frequency, Weather proof IP 67 Cast Al compact housing. PNP DC output

3. HONEYBEE 7230 - 2Khz frequency, Weather proof IP 68 C Steel Pipe housing. PNP DC output

4. HONEYBEE 7220 Ex - 2K Hz frequency, Weather proof IP,65, Flameproof as per BIS standard vide CIMFRTc/sR/H642 DT16tA2t20 and PESO certificate no A/P/HQ/MH/104/2738(P302245)

The Aqua Fork Point Level Switch is a cutting-edge device designed to provide accurate and reliable liquid level detection in a variety of industrial applications. Whether you need to monitor the level of water, oil, chemicals, or other liquids, this innovative switch offers a robust solution to ensure efficient and safe operations.

Details

  • Housing : Steel/ Aluminum, weather proof,powder coated. Integral with the probe
  • Cable entry : 1 nos.( standard)
  • Ambient temperature : 0 ° C to +60° C
  • Power consumption : 0.3VA
  • Mains Voltage : 18 to 36 V DC or
  • Output : PNP or NPN DC output.
  • Switching delay : Continuously adjustable From 2 (in cast Al Version) to 20 sec. probe Free or probe covered
  • Safety : Field selected switch over for(In cast Al version) min or max(FSL/FSH) Switching points
  • Switch status display : Green LED shows Normal, Red LED shows alarm

Application

  • Most liquids with max 10000 mm2/s viscosity.

Features

Fork Design : The Aqua Fork Point Level Switch features a unique fork design with two prongs extending into the liquid. When the liquid level reaches the fork, it creates a change in capacitance, triggering the switch to indicate the presence or absence of liquid. This design eliminates the need for moving parts or floats, minimizing maintenance requirements and enhancing durability.

Wide Liquid Compatibility : This level switch is compatible with a wide range of liquids, including water, oil, chemicals, and viscous fluids. Its versatile design makes it suitable for various industries such as wastewater treatment, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and oil and gas.

Easy Installation : The Aqua Fork Point Level Switch is designed for easy installation and integration into existing systems. It can be mounted on tanks, vessels, or pipes using standard fittings, making it convenient to incorporate into your industrial processes.

Robust Construction : Built to withstand harsh environments, this level switch is constructed from durable materials such as stainless steel, ensuring longevity and resistance to corrosion. It is designed to operate reliably even in challenging conditions, making it ideal for use in demanding industrial settings.

Adjustable Sensitivity : The sensitivity of the Aqua Fork Point Level Switch can be easily adjusted to suit specific application requirements. Whether you need to detect high or low liquid levels, the switch can be fine-tuned to provide accurate and timely signals.

Output Options : The Aqua Fork Point Level Switch offers various output options to suit different system configurations. It can provide simple on/off signals for basic level detection or more advanced output signals such as analog or digital signals for integration with control systems.

Easy Maintenance : Thanks to its non-contact design and robust construction, the Aqua Fork Point Level Switch requires minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning is straightforward, and the absence of moving parts eliminates the need for frequent replacements or repairs.

In conclusion, the Aqua Fork Point Level Switch is a reliable and efficient solution for liquid level detection in industrial applications. With its unique fork design, wide liquid compatibility, high accuracy, and easy installation, this level switch ensures smooth and safe operations while minimizing downtime and maintenance costs. Whether you need to monitor liquid levels in tanks, pipes, or vessels, the Aqua Fork Point Level Switch offers a dependable and cost-effective solution.
