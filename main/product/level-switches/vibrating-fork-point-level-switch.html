Vibrating Fork Point Level Switches

Download Literature

A tuning fork made of SS 316 L is kept vibrating at its resonant frequency of approx. 125 Hz(standard) by piezo ceramic elements. when the service material covers the tines of the fork, vibrations are damped. This damping is sensed electronically, and the processed signal is used to energize a relay whose contact in turn are used for control.
